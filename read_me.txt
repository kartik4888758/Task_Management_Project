# Simple Task Management Program

This is a straightforward Python program that allows you to manage your tasks with ease. Think of it as your digital to-do list.

## Features

- **Add Tasks**: You can add tasks to your list by giving them a title and description. Just like writing down what you need to do.

- **View Tasks**: The program shows you all your tasks, so you can see what's on your list.

- **Mark Tasks Done**: When you complete a task, let the program know, and it will remember it for you.

- **Delete Tasks**: If you no longer need a task, you can remove it from your list.

- **Automatic Saving**: Your tasks are automatically saved, so you won't lose them when you close the program. They're kept in a special file called `tasks.json`.

## How to Use

1. Run the program in your Python environment.
2. You'll see a menu with options like "Add Task," "View Tasks," and more. Choose what you want to do from the menu.
3. Follow the prompts to add, view, complete, or delete tasks.
4. When you're done, select "Exit" to save your tasks and close the program.

## Why Use This Program?

- It's easy to use and perfect for managing your daily tasks.
- Your tasks are saved, so they're always there when you need them.
- No need for fancy apps; this program does the job simply and effectively.

Feel free to download, customize, and use this program to keep track of your tasks and stay organized!
